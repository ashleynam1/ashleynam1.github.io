Why ReMindful?

You care about the planet. You mean to bring your reusable bag or bottle — but life gets busy.

ReMindful is your friendly tap on the shoulder the moment you step out the door. Whether you're heading to the store, gym, or just grabbing coffee, a smart notification reminds you: “Grab your reusables.”

It’s simple. It’s seamless. And it helps you stick to the habits you actually want.

How It Works

📍 Set Your Spot

Add your home as a location trigger.

🔗 Connect IFTTT

Link location services with Pushbullet.

🔔 Get Reminded

As you head out, receive a smart push: “Bring your reusable bag!”
